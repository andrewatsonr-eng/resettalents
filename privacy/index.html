Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2, 2026

1. Who We Are

Reset Talents LLC ("Reset Talents," "we," "us," or "our") is a full-service event production company based in New York City. We operate the website resettalents.com. Our contact email is info@resettalents.com.

2. Information We Collect

When you use our website or contact us, we may collect the following information:

  • Contact information — your name and email address when you submit a contact or event request form.
  • Event information — event date, location, type, guest count, and services requested.
  • Communications — any messages or notes you include in your forms.
  • Usage data — basic analytics about how visitors interact with our website (pages visited, browser type, device type). We do not use this to identify individuals.

We do not collect payment information. We do not collect any information from minors under 18.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide solely to:

  • Respond to your event inquiry and send you a personalized quote.
  • Contact vendors on your behalf to confirm availability and pricing.
  • Follow up regarding your event booking.
  • Improve our website and services based on aggregate usage patterns.

We do not use your information for marketing purposes without your explicit consent. We do not sell or rent your personal information to any third party.

4. How We Share Your Information

Your information may be shared in the following limited circumstances:

  • Vendors and partners — we share relevant event details (date, location, type, guest count) with vendors we contact on your behalf to confirm availability and obtain quotes. We share the minimum information necessary.
  • Legal requirements — we may disclose information if required by law or to protect our legal rights.

We do not share your contact details with vendors without your knowledge as part of the event request process.

5. Data Retention

We retain your event inquiry information for as long as necessary to fulfill your booking and for reasonable business record-keeping purposes. You may request deletion of your information at any time by emailing info@resettalents.com.

6. Cookies

Our website may use basic cookies for site functionality and analytics. We do not use advertising cookies or track you across other websites. You can disable cookies in your browser settings at any time without affecting your ability to use our site.

7. Third-Party Links

Our website contains links to third-party websites including venue partners, SoundCloud, and other services. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites. We encourage you to review their privacy policies separately.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal information we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate information.
  • Request deletion of your information.
  • Withdraw consent for us to contact you at any time.

To exercise any of these rights, please email us at info@resettalents.com.

9. Security

We take reasonable precautions to protect your information. Our website is hosted on Cloudflare Pages, which provides SSL encryption for all data transmitted between your browser and our site. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect any changes. Continued use of our website after updates constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.
