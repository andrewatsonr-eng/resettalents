The Process

From brief to your SoundCloud in 5 steps.

01
Tell Us About Your Space

Share your venue type, brand identity, clientele, operating hours, and any reference music you already love. The more you tell us, the better we craft your sound.

02
We Match You With Your DJ

From our roster of hundreds of talented DJs, we select the artist whose style, genre expertise, and sensibility is the perfect match for your brand.

03
Your Mix Is Crafted

The DJ curates and records a bespoke long-form set — a genuine musical journey with seamless transitions, no gaps, and a narrative arc designed for your space and crowd.

04
Delivered to Your SoundCloud

The finished mix is uploaded directly to your SoundCloud account. Private or public — your choice. Play from any device connected to your sound system, instantly.

05
Refresh on Your Schedule

Get new mixes weekly, monthly, or seasonally. Your library grows, your regulars always hear something fresh, and your brand's sound evolves with you.
