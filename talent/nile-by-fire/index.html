DJ · Event Curator · Bar Director
Nile by Fire
Nile Stevens · Brooklyn, New York

A cross-genre selector working across House, Dance, Open Format, Hip Hop and R&B. His signature is sexy and fun — high-energy, body-moving sets threaded with interesting sonic flavors layered in from across the world. He runs and spins the monthly LMSS party at Speak Softly, the hidden Chelsea speakeasy beneath LouLou, and has built events and DJ'd across both New York and Miami. Off the decks, he brings 16+ years in high-end hospitality and is currently the Operations Manager & Bar Director at LouLou, Chelsea.

The rare artist who shapes both the sound of a room — and what's in your glass.
